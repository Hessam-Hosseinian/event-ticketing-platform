# Product vision
For ticket buyers and organizers facing unreliable flash sales, SeatFair is a transparent
event marketplace that combines live inventory, fair queue admission, safe payment, and
instant verifiable tickets. Unlike a basic CRUD marketplace, it treats concurrency and
failure recovery as product features.

Personas are buyers, organizers, venue operators, and support administrators. Success is
measured by zero oversells, checkout success rate above 98%, p95 catalog latency below
500 ms, notification delivery above 99%, and a measurable queue-to-purchase conversion.
